What are human needs?

Human needs can be fit into different categories such as: wants, needs and others

Some 'needs' which are necessary to survival are:

  • Different nutritious foods
  • Clean drinking water
  • Proper medication/healthcare
  • Therapy/Support

But examples of 'wants' are:

  • Electronics
  • Entertainment
  • Cars
  • Holidays
  • Furniture

Some of these can be debated as the human world progresses, certian things will be needed

for said person to live a healthy lifestyle with a job, home and car (to get to work and other places)
